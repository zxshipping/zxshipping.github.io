About Us

ZX Shipping

Connecting families and businesses between the U.S. and Haiti

Our Story

Founded in Naples, Florida, ZX Shipping was born out of a need for reliable, affordable shipping services between the United States and Haiti. As members of the Haitian diaspora ourselves, we understand the importance of staying connected with family and friends back home.

What started as a small operation helping local community members send packages to loved ones has grown into a trusted shipping service with customers across the United States.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make shipping to Haiti as simple, fast, and affordable as possible. We're committed to providing excellent customer service and transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

We pride ourselves on our Haitian roots and our understanding of the unique challenges of shipping to the country. This local knowledge allows us to navigate customs efficiently and ensure your packages arrive safely.

Our Location

ZX Shipping is located in Naples, Florida, where we accept package drop-offs and forward shipments to Haiti. Our convenient location serves customers throughout Florida and accepts packages shipped from anywhere in the U.S.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about shipping to Haiti

How long does shipping take?
Packages typically arrive in Haiti within 5-10 business days from when we receive them in our Florida facility. Express shipping is available with delivery in 3-7 business days.
What airports in Haiti do you ship to?
We ship to Toussaint Louverture International Airport in Port-au-Prince (PAP HT) and Cap-Haïtien International Airport (CAP HT). You can specify your preferred destination when creating your shipment.
How is pricing calculated?
We charge by weight (per pound) or dimensional weight (for lightweight but large packages), whichever is greater. Our current rates are $3.50/lb for standard shipping and $4.50/lb for express.
What items are prohibited?
We cannot ship hazardous materials, weapons, ammunition, perishable foods, live animals, or illegal items. Some restrictions apply to electronics, medications, and other regulated goods.
How does the shopping service work?
You provide us with links to items you want to purchase from U.S. retailers. We buy them for you (charging your payment method), receive them at our facility, then ship them to Haiti. A 10% service fee (minimum $10) applies.
Can I drop off packages in person?
Yes, we accept package drop-offs at our Naples, Florida location during business hours. You can also ship packages to us from anywhere in the U.S. using any carrier.
